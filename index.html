I am a Research Associate in the Department of Computer Science & Engineering at the University of Minnesota. My research focuses on autonomous systems, teleoperation, robotics, multimodal perception, and network-aware intelligent systems for real-world deployment.

My recent work explores scalable remote driving systems, AV-edge collaborative perception, VLM-driven reasoning for intervention and escalation, and communication-aware safety systems for rural transportation and robotic autonomy. I am particularly interested in how perception, networking, and human-in-the-loop intelligence can be jointly designed to improve reliability and practical deployment.
